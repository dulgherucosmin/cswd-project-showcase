Our Mission

Paws for Effect's goal is to provide cats & dogs with veterinary care, homes, spays/neutering and to help owners by providing education and outreaching programmes. If you wish to support us in any way possible we have a guide on how you can help us below.

ADOPT FROM US

We offer adoption options for both cats and dogs! View some of our animals up for adoption below. You just might find the perfect pet for you!

HOW TO HELP

Donate

We rely on generous donations from the public to support our work.

Fundraise

You can fundraise at events and meetings to help us rescue, rehabilitate and give these sick, injured, cruelly treated animals new homes.

Adopt

Adopting from us gives these cats new homes and a second chance at happy lives.

Volunteer

We offer volunteering options for people who want to help personally with our mission.
